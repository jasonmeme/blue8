Our History

Blue VIII is a team with 4 years of experience in the FIRST Tech Challenge, with previous experience in FIRST through FIRST Lego League. As a team, we strive for perfection, professionalism, and positive growth; we seek to learn from the mistakes we made and the experiences we gained throughout the season. We live by the design methodology PDCA to test and improve our robot and code. We also help mentor First Lego League and Jr. First Lego League teams to promote STEM and foster young communities of learning.
